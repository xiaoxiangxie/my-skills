# Industry Angle Library

Use this when the product category is not already covered by game or social-feed patterns. Pick one primary angle and one backup angle before storyboarding.

## SaaS and AI Tools

- Hook: show the result first, then collapse the old workflow.
- Visual: real UI screenshot, task cards completing, agent/chat actions becoming product output.
- Proof: integration count, time saved, waitlist, customer quote, or user-supplied result.
- Avoid: abstract glowing dashboards with no product behavior.

## Ecommerce and Physical Products

- Hook: tactile problem, messy before, or satisfying transformation.
- Visual: product close-up, use-in-context, before/after, bundle reveal, comparison grid.
- Proof: materials, rating, review, guarantee, shipping/offer when source-backed.
- Avoid: generic product beauty shots without a use case.

## Local Services

- Hook: urgent local problem or calendar/time pressure.
- Visual: location cue, booking flow, technician/service moment, map pin, before/after.
- Proof: review score, response time, service area, license/insurance only if source-backed.
- Avoid: broad brand film language.

## Education and Courses

- Hook: specific skill gap or outcome preview.
- Visual: lesson snippet, progress path, project/result reveal, instructor credibility.
- Proof: curriculum, completion asset, user-supplied student result, certification if verified.
- Avoid: income or job guarantees unless approved.

## Health, Fitness, and Wellness

- Hook: routine friction or visible form/behavior improvement.
- Visual: safe movement demo, habit tracker, product-in-use, progress cues.
- Proof: trainer credential, user-supplied testimonial, app rating.
- Avoid: medical claims, body-shaming, or guaranteed health outcomes.

## Finance, Legal, Insurance, and Regulated Offers

- Hook: clarity, speed, or control, not fear-mongering.
- Visual: calculators, document flow, comparison, checklist, secure workflow.
- Proof: rates, returns, legal outcomes, coverage, or savings only when approved.
- Avoid: rendering inferred claims. Use disclaimers and conservative copy.

## B2B Operations

- Hook: operational bottleneck, manual handoff, or failed status board.
- Visual: pipeline, queue, alert, approval, spreadsheet-to-system transformation.
- Proof: SLA, error reduction, team adoption, integration, or user-supplied metric.
- Avoid: consumer-style hype that hides the workflow.

## Creative and Productivity Apps

- Hook: blank page becomes finished output.
- Visual: timeline, canvas, editor controls, asset generation, export moment.
- Proof: templates, export formats, collaboration, rating, creator quote.
- Avoid: showing only final art without the creation interaction.

## Default Pattern

If no category fits, use:

1. Problem or desire in the first 2 seconds.
2. Product visible immediately.
3. One concrete product behavior.
4. One source-backed proof point.
5. CTA with a specific next action.
